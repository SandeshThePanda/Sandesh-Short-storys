What is Global warming?

Global warming refers to long-term shifts in temperatures and weather patterns. These shifts may be natural, but since the 1800s,
human activities have been the main driver of climate change, primarily due to the burning of fossil fuels (like coal, oil and gas), which produces heat-trapping gases which makes the temperatur go higher and higher.
